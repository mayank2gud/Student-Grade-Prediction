Welcome to Students Grade Predictor

This project aims at developing a highly effective rank/grades predictor of a student.
We here come up with a system where student final grade is predicted based on the marks he had scored during his previous course and years. In order to predict the grade of the student we need some data to analyze and to predict the grade. So we will input student basic information and their academic information into the system which will be used to predict the grade of the student
